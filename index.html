ABOUT ME

My name is Dorothy and I am a Year 3 student major in Accountancy. I am a "contradictory" person, cauze my ideas are full of contradictions. For example, I like studying Accounting, but somehow I don't want to be an accountant; I am interested in programming, coding and anything about a computer (that's why I chose to minor in Computing!), but I know I don't like being a programmer. The most contradictory thing is I am poor and I need money, but I don't want to find any jobs! What I want is just staying at my home, receiving money without working for 8+ hours ~ 😂

I'm a quiet person so my hobbies are basically indoor activities like listening to music, reading novels, watching movies & dramas, cooking, singing, etc. I also like "playing music instrument". However due to economic factor (i.e. poor), I've never learnt any types of music instrument untill last month I bought a Ukulele and stated to learn. So, I can only "play" the virtual piano on the Internet. 😂
